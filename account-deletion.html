Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Plan B account and associated data, please send a request using the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at: silicongeni@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: Plan B - Delete My Account
  3. Include the email address associated with your account

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information (email, display name)
  • Stored chat history
  • Usage and activity records linked to your account

Deletion Timeline

All data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of verifying your request.

Legal Retention

If certain data must be retained for legal or security purposes, it will be stored only as required by law and deleted afterward.
